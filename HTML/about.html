About Kahoro

We are a trusted provider of high-quality MDF boards, photo mounts, frames, photobooks, and custom decor solutions. Our commitment to craftsmanship and client satisfaction has made us a leading brand in the industry.

Our Journey

2015 - The Beginning

Started as a small framing workshop in Nairobi, serving a few local photographers.

2018 - Growth

Expanded into photo mounts, customized MDF boards, and added a new production line.

2022 - Digital Transition

Launched our online catalog and started fulfilling orders via WhatsApp and social media.

2024 - Today

We serve hundreds of clients across Kenya with photo books, wall mounts, and framing products.
